Who We Are

More Than A Club

Rustnuts SMC is a social motorcycle club based in Greater Melbourne, Victoria. We started the way the best clubs do — a few mates and their machines — and grew into a family where riders connect, look out for one another and break down the isolation too many people carry on their own. We're a place to drop the mask, twist the throttle and belong.
