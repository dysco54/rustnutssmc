Who We Are

More Than A Club

Rustnuts SMC is a social motorcycle club based in Greater Melbourne, Victoria. We started the way the best clubs do — a few mates and their machines — and grew into a community where riders connect, look out for one another and break down the isolation too many people carry on their own. We're a place to drop the mask, twist the throttle and belong.

Riding For A Reason

Causes We Back

Every season we put the kilometres to work, raising money and awareness for causes close to the club.

Men's Mental Health

Connection over isolation. Supporting riders and their families, and the conversations that keep mates around.

Breast Cancer Awareness

Our annual awareness ride — registration, poker run and a show 'n' shine to finish — raising funds and turning heads.

Autism Awareness

Riding in support of autism awareness alongside friends and fellow clubs across Victoria.

West & North

Chapter: West  ·  Western Melbourne

Registration & check-in, group ride, poker run and a show 'n' shine finish with trophies for Best Cruiser, Best Sports and Best Pre-1990.
