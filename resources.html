Support & Resources

No one rides alone. Whether it's your head, the law, or the road — here are the people who can help. Reaching out is strength, not weakness.

In an emergency

If you or someone else is in immediate danger, call 000 (Triple Zero) now.

Mental Health & Crisis

24/7 Support

Free, confidential support — any time, day or night. You don't have to be in crisis to call. If things feel heavy, these people are there to listen.

Lifeline
13 11 14
24/7 crisis support and suicide prevention. Call or text 0477 13 11 14.
MensLine Australia
1300 78 99 78
24/7 telephone and online counselling for men — mental health, relationships, anger, stress and wellbeing.
Beyond Blue
1300 22 4636
24/7 support for depression, anxiety and related conditions, plus information and referrals.
Suicide Call Back Service
1300 659 467
Free 24/7 phone and online counselling for anyone at risk, bereaved, or worried about someone.
SuicideLine Victoria
1300 651 251
Victorian 24/7 telephone and online counselling for anyone affected by suicide or emotional distress.
Black Dog Institute
Online resources
Evidence-based mental health tools, self-tests and resources. A cause close to the club.

Legal Help

Know Your Rights

Whether it's a road accident, a TAC claim, or family matters — get proper advice early. The right lawyer makes all the difference.

A firm that's helped our members
Grainger Legal
graingerlegal.com.au
Regional Victorian personal injury firm (Castlemaine, Bacchus Marsh, Sunbury) specialising in TAC claims, WorkCover, public liability and TPD — on a No Win, No Fee basis. They've looked after our own when it mattered.
TAC Victoria
1300 654 329
The Transport Accident Commission — lodge and manage a claim after a road accident in Victoria.
Victoria Legal Aid
1300 792 387
Free legal information and advice, including family law. A good first port of call.
Find a Lawyer (LIV)
Law Institute of Victoria
Official referral service to find an accredited Victorian lawyer for family law and other matters.

Family & Wellbeing

Support At Home

Support that reaches beyond the rider — for families, finances and the tough stuff.

1800RESPECT
1800 737 732
24/7 confidential support for anyone affected by domestic, family or sexual violence. Text 0458 737 732.
Men's Referral Service
1300 766 491
24/7 support for men wanting to change controlling or violent behaviour. A positive first step.
National Debt Helpline
1800 007 007
Free, confidential financial counselling. Weekdays 9:30am–4:30pm.
Gambling Help Online
1800 858 858
Free 24/7 support, chat and counselling for anyone affected by gambling.

These resources are shared in good faith to help our community find support. Rustnuts SMC is not affiliated with most of these organisations and provides these links for information only — they are not a substitute for professional advice. Phone numbers and details were correct at the time of listing; please confirm directly with each service. In an emergency, always call 000.
